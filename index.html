Our Story

Bringing Greece to London

Myrtos is the culmination of a lifelong dream for Chef Asimakis Chaniotis. After achieving international acclaim and Michelin stars, he returned to his roots to create a restaurant that speaks directly to his Greek heritage.

Located in the heart of London, Myrtos is more than a restaurant; it is an extension of the Greek dining table. Our mission is to bring the warmth of Greek hospitality and the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean to the city.

Quality

Sourcing the finest ingredients from Greek producers and local London markets.

Authenticity

Staying true to traditional Greek flavors while embracing modern culinary techniques.

Hospitality

Treating every guest like family, offering an experience that nourishes the soul.
